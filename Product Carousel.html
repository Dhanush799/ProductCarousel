A product is a tangible or intangible item offered for sale that satisfies a need or want. In the realm of commerce, products encompass a wide array of goods and services, ranging from physical objects like electronics, clothing, and furniture to intangible offerings such as software, consultancy, and entertainment. The concept of a product extends beyond its physical attributes, encompassing aspects like functionality, design, brand, and overall user experience. Successful products are often characterized by their ability to meet consumer needs, provide value, and differentiate themselves in a competitive market. The development and marketing of products involve various stages, from conceptualization and design to production, distribution, and customer support. Ultimately, a product's success is closely tied to its ability to resonate with the target audience and deliver a solution or experience that enhances the lives of its users.
